Knowledge Representation and Reasoning

A study that encompasses the examination and exploration of techniques, principles, and methodologies for representing and processing knowledge, with a primary focus on enabling intelligent behavior and enhancing communication between humans and machines in the domain of Artificial Intelligence.

Module Aims

  1. Discuss how to define knowledge and explain the concept of KRR and its relationship to data and information.
  2. Gain an appreciation of the importance and rationale for employing formal approaches to KRR.
  3. Demonstrate an understanding of the basics of logic and its application to KRR problems.
  4. Acquire an understanding of the professional competence required to able be to identify and examine KRR technologies for Artificial Intelligence applications.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Critique the need for formal approaches to knowledge representation and reasoning.
  2. Review critically properties of a knowledge-based system.
  3. Appraise critically modelling techniques for knowledge representation and reasoning.
  4. Examine and incorporate different modelling approaches to solving KRR problems.
